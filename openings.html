Openings

 

1. Postdoctoral researchers  

We are seeking a highly motivated and talented postdoctoral researcher to join our team. The successful candidate will contribute to projects at the intersection of computational chemistry and machine learning. Outstanding candidates will be nominated for the RGC Postdoctoral Fellowship Scheme. Please email xinglong.zhang@cuhk.edu.hk with your CV, statement of motivation and 3 representative publications to apply. More details can be found here or on CUHK Jobpage here.

2. PhD students   [Positions Filled for Fall 2025 Entry]

We invite creative, inquisitive and motivated students to apply for our postgraduate programme to join our team and start in Fall 2025. Students may be funded with Postgraduate Studentships. Outstanding candidates will stand to compete for the prestigious Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS). Please email xinglong.zhang@cuhk.edu.hk with your CV, IELTS/TOFEL results (if available) and names and contact information of 3 referees to apply. More details can be found here.

Possible Sources of Funding:

  • Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) (Open for 2025 Intake)
    This is a highly prestigious and competitive funding for PhD studies in HK. The HKPFS usually calls for application from 1 September, with a deadline on 1 December (at 12:00 noon, Hong Kong time) every year. Application result will be notified in March the following year.
  • CUHK Vice-Chancellor's PhD Scholarship Scheme (Open for 2025 Intake)
  • Postgraduate Studentships (MPhil and PhD) (Open for 2025 Intake)
More details please consult CUHK graduate admission website here.

3. Masters, undergraduate, high school students and exchange students  

Our lab has internship opportunities for pre-doctoral and pre-university (high school) students and exchange opportunities for PhD students in Fall 2025. Please email xinglong.zhang@cuhk.edu.hk with your CV and names and contact information of 3 referees to apply.

4. Academic visitors  

We welcome all academic visitors to our group starting Fall 2025. Please email xinglong.zhang@cuhk.edu.hk to discuss further.

Please get in touch with us xinglong.zhang@cuhk.edu.hk if you have your own source of funding and would like to join our group in any capacity (e.g., as an academic visitor or exchange student).
