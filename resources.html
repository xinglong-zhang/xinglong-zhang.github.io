For Undergraduates


I must duly acknowledge that some of the materials are drawn freely from my undergraduate lecture notes. Where the explanations in the notes are unclear, or too brief, to the point where the actual derivations become difficult for some students, I attempt to make this clearer by explaining using my own reasoning. It should be noted, however, that any mistakes herein are mine and mine alone. Please send any suggestions and/or corrections to xinglong.zhang@cuhk.edu.hk.
